The racing branch of Ferrari was created by Enzo Ferrari, an entrepreneur and race driver. Ferrari first entered Formula One in 1950, entering the Monaco Grand Prix that year. Ferrari are the most successful racing team in Formula One history. Drivers have won 15 championships, with Ferrari themselves winning 16 constructors’ championships Ferrari have claimed 239 race victories out of 1032 race entries, with drivers reaching the podium in 778 of those races. Ferarri’s current drivers for the 2023 season are Charles Leclerc and Carlos Sainz.
